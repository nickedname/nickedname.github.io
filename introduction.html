Game Mechanics

For my course Game Mechanics I've made this portfolio site and a prototype of a game.
The game will be playtested throughout the development process.
The game plot revolves around a hero whose princess gets stolen by great evil(mother in law).
The game mechanics revolve around solving puzzles while sustaining the flame on your character.
Enemies are introduced from easy to hard difficulty.
Upon reaching the final boss you can use your knowledge against it.
Unfortunately I had to remove parts of the code, because Unity had issues upon building.
The boss fight also didn't make it and it's concept shall be remembered.
