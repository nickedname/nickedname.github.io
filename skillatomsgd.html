Skill Atom Diagram

As an assignment for Game Mechanics I have made a Skill Atom Diagram.
Each Skill Atom is made of the following properties:
Title of the atom in the blue section.
The action a player takes indicated by a flag image(due to lack of a better button image).
The simulation that occurs as a result of said action, indicated by a cog wheel/gear.
The feedback the player receives, indicated by an eye.
The skill the player has (hopefully) learned.
